Heart Risk Predictor

Your one-stop tool for assessing heart health and risks with ease.

Project Abstract

The Heart Disease Classification project aims to predict the risk of heart disease using the Random Forest Algorithm. This machine learning model analyzes various health parameters to provide accurate risk assessments. The prediction dashboard allows users to input their health data and receive instant feedback on their heart disease risk level, along with personalized recommendations for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
